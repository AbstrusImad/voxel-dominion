Field manual

How the world turns, from the first voxel to the falling of an age.

01

Raise a structure

Open the Foundry and place voxels block by block. Every coordinate from 0 to 15, every one of the eight materials, is yours to command. Three blocks is the minimum, two hundred fifty-six the limit.

02

The World Spirit judges fit to the age

Submit your work against a parcel. An on-chain AI weighs the structure against the living era, the rule it rewards and the form it favors, and returns a verdict: Masterwork, Worthy, Weak, or Reject, with a score.

03

Beat the standing score to capture

A claim is seized when the verdict is Worthy or Masterwork, the score is at least fifty, and it exceeds the parcel current score. Fall short and the holder keeps the ground.

04

Ages turn and erode claims

As captures accumulate the age matures. Anyone may turn it. When it turns, the rule changes and erosion weakens every standing claim, opening the map to fresh conquest.

05

Consensus rules every verdict

A leader drafts the judgment, then validators independently re-run it. They compare the verdict exactly and the score within tolerance. Only a ruling that survives consensus is written to the chain.

06

You pay only network fees

There is no entry price and no stake. Each transaction costs ordinary Bradbury Testnet fees, most of which are refunded. Claim test GEN from the faucet to begin.
